Broadstreet COVID-19 Data Project

Analytics Team

The analytics team analyzes COVID-19 Race and Ethnicity data that is uploaded on our GitHub. A recent project that they looked into was determining if season affects COVID-19 case rates for race and ethnicity. Using personal experiences, they had predicted together that COVID-19 case rates would be higher for all races and ethnicities in the winter months (defined as November 2020 to January 2021) because many people would be travelling back home. Each person on the team chose a county and used the Race and Ethnicity Data to further test this hypothesis. We have attached the code (and the corresponding presentations) to the following counties:

  • Los Angeles County, California - analyzed by Kavya Rajaputhra
  • Hennepin County, MN - analyzed by Brandon Voigt
  • New York City, New York - analyzed by Sarandeep Kaur
  • Boone County, Indiana - analyzed by Francis Pacillo

The code for each of the projects is labelled “Name - county Seasonality Code” and the presentation is labelled “Name - county Presentation”.
